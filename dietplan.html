Meal Plan for Weight Loss in Malaysia

Breakfast : Nasi Lemak with Egg and Vegetables

Typically, pandan leaves, ginger, garlic, fenugreek, white jasmine, and bay leaves are cooked with rice in coconut milk to make this meal. The smell of this breakfast will blow you away, and it's a powerful meal that will keep you going all day. Start your day with a Malaysian classic, Nasi Lemak but prepared with a healthy twist. This version uses low-fat milk and includes boiled eggs and fresh vegetables to keep you energized throughout the day.

Ingredients Portions
8-10 Tablespoons of cooked rice 2 starchs
1 cup teh tarik with stevia (no sugar) 1/2 dairy
2 boiled egg 1 protein
1/2 cucumber 1 vegetable

Mid-morning snack : Fruit Bowl

A refreshing fruit bowl packed with diced papaya and pineapple is perfect to satisfy your sweet tooth while providing essential nutrients, fiber, and a natural energy boost for the rest of your morning. Papaya has a lot of fiber, which might help you lose weight, while pineapple has a lot of antioxidants. Your immunity will be strengthened by this fruit dish.

Ingredients Portions
Diced papaya and pineapple 2 fruits

Lunch : Chicken satay with Thai peanut sauce

How could we overlook include everyone's favorite chicken satay in your menu?
Enjoy a hearty and flavorful lunch with tender grilled chicken satay paired with Thai peanut sauce. This dish is oil-free and pairs perfectly with a healthier version of Roti Canai, offering a satisfying and balanced meal.This delicious meal will lift your spirits.

Ingredients Portions
Roti Canai cooked in 1 teaspoon oil 2 starchs an 1 fat
1 cup kopi susu with stevia (no sugar) 1/2 dairy
6 ounces of grilled chicken 2 proteins
1 cup vegetable salad (cabbage, carrot, broccolli) 1 vegetable

Evening Snacks : Soy Milk with Nuts

Recharge your energy with a simple yet nourishing snack of soy milk and a handful of mixed nuts. This combination is rich in calcium, omega-3 fatty acids, and protein to keep you feeling full and healthy.

Ingredients Portions
1 cup milk 1 dairy
1 handful of mixed nuts 1 nuts

Dinner : Fish curry with brown rice

Sticking to lean protein is the aim of this meal plan. It aids in gaining muscle growth without causing the body to accumulate more fat. The greatest source of fat-free protein is fish, which a real Malaysian must eat ! Thus, use spices like turmeric, ginger, and kaffir lime leaf to make fish curry for supper. You will be able to taste more flavors thanks to them. Serve it with pieces of cucumber.

Ingredients Portions
2/3 cup or 8-10 tablespoons boiled brown rice 2 starchs
1 cup kopi susu with stevia (no sugar) 1 dairy
6 ounces of grilled fish & 1 fried egg in 2 teaspoons oil 2 proteins
Sambal & cucumber 2 vegetables
